<ul>
  <li><strong>document-like:</strong> follows strong rules and has implicit context</li>
  <li><strong>object-centric:</strong> objects can be defined freely and have to be included explicitely in builds</li>
  <li><strong>script-centric:</strong> declares a sequence of actions</li>
  <li><strong>preprocessed:</strong></li>
  <li><strong>fluent:</strong> uses chained methods to represent more complex structures</li>
  <li><strong>abstract:</strong> is independent of specific implementations in Minecraft</li>
</ul>
